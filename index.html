Common Questions
Everything you need to know before getting started.
What broker does Omega Titan work with? +
Omega Titan connects to OANDA via their REST API. You need an OANDA fxTrade account (practice or live). We support fxTrade Practice for paper trading and fxTrade Live for real capital. No MetaTrader required.
What are the VPS requirements? +
Any Linux VPS with Python 3.10+ and 512MB RAM. We recommend a $5–$12/month DigitalOcean or Vultr droplet. The bot runs as a systemd service with automatic restart on failure and auto-start on reboot. We help you set everything up.
Can I run this on my personal computer? +
Technically yes, but we strongly recommend a VPS for 24/5 uptime. The forex market runs around the clock during the week. Closing your laptop means missing trades. A VPS ensures the bot never sleeps.
How is the code protected? +
Five layers of protection: compiled bytecode (no readable source), hardware-locked license keys, encrypted parameter vault, anti-tampering integrity checks, and debugger detection. Each license is bound to a single machine — it cannot be copied, shared, or reverse-engineered.
Do you guarantee profits? +
No. No trading system can guarantee profits. Forex trading carries significant risk, and past algorithmic performance does not guarantee future results. Omega Titan is a sophisticated analysis and execution tool — not a money printer. We recommend starting on a practice account.
What makes this different from other trading bots? +
Most bots rely on combinations of RSI, MACD, and moving average crossovers — strategies that stopped working consistently years ago. Omega Titan uses scientific models from entirely different fields: swarm intelligence, seismic wave analysis, computational immunology, and information entropy. These signals are fundamentally uncorrelated with conventional technical indicators.
What pairs does it trade? +
Currently optimized for EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, and GBP/JPY on the H1 and M30 timeframes. Each pair has individually calibrated parameters. Enterprise clients can request custom pair configurations.
